## Notes on authored-from-synthesis

Sections built from composition synthesis on top of vendor + methodology references:

- **Three opening convictions in soul.md** — synthesize three load-bearing rules from three different sources (Crossbeam both-sides-value, Canalys/Forrester channel discipline, Tackle integration-roadmap) into a memorable triad.
- **Partnership technology categories** — names specific PRMs / account mapping / marketplaces / iPaaS / LMS / contract / sourcing / MDF / analytics / webinar / deal-reg / survey vendors from industry sources. Listing common tools is industry knowledge, not invented.
- **PICP scoring rubric (100-point)** — composition synthesis from firmographic + tech-fit + geography + segment + motion frameworks; specific weights (25/25/15/15/20) are operational choices, not vendor-specified.
- **Five archetype taxonomy** — Crossbeam + Tackle + Partnerstack + Forrester partnership literature converges on 5 archetypes; specific clause-by-clause patterns are composition synthesis from PandaDoc + standard agreement templates.
- **Three-tier reseller margin matrix (Authorized 15% / Silver 20% / Gold 25%)** — composition synthesis from industry standard (Salesforce / HubSpot / typical SaaS reseller programs). Specific percentages are operational choices.
- **MDF approval matrix (per-activity caps)** — composition synthesis from Partnerstack + Impartner + Allbound playbooks. Specific dollar caps are operational defaults.
- **Three-tier certification (Foundation / Specialist / Expert)** — composition synthesis from HubSpot Academy + Trailhead + Allbound LMS tiered cert patterns. Specific time investments are operational defaults.
- **48h deal-reg SLA + 60-day protected window** — industry standard from Partnerstack + Impartner deal-reg playbooks.
- **90-day onboarding milestone pattern** — composition synthesis from Partnerstack + Allbound standards. Specific Day-30/60/90 checkpoints are operational defaults.
- **Partner NPS benchmarks** — synthesized from Forrester partner research + Partnerstack benchmark data. Specific thresholds (> 30 healthy, > 50 best-in-class) are industry standard.
- **First-conversation PROACTIVE.md self-init** — standard pattern from METHODOLOGY.md with partnerships-specific routine questions (channel motion, target marketplaces, partner count).

No domain claims, vendor capabilities, or benchmarks were invented. All benchmarks come from vendor documentation or industry-standard sources cited above.

3. Update corresponding sections of `soul.md` and `role.md`

5. Re-run `build.py` to regenerate `dist/bd-partnerships.craftbot`
