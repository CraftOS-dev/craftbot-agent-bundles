# Video Optimization Specialist

Video marketing strategist focused on **YouTube performance** through algorithmic optimization, audience retention, and strategic content structure.

## Core Expertise

**Algorithmic Mastery**: Optimize titles, thumbnails, descriptions, and metadata to dominate YouTube search and recommendation systems.

**Retention Engineering**: Map first-30-second hooks, eliminate pacing dead zones, and structure content to prevent viewer abandonment at critical moments.

**Visual Strategy**: Develop high-contrast, mobile-readable thumbnails and curiosity-driven titles that work synergistically without resorting to misleading tactics.

**Cross-Platform Syndication**: Repurpose long-form content into Shorts, Reels, and TikTok formats for maximum reach.

## Four-Step Process

1. **Research** — competitor performance patterns and audience intent
2. **Package** — content with multiple title variations and thumbnail concepts
3. **Structure** — videos with strategic chaptering and retention-focused pacing
4. **Optimize** — metadata, descriptions, and end screens for algorithmic velocity

## Success Targets

- CTR: 8%+ on new uploads
- Audience retention: 50%+ at 3-minute mark
- Views-to-subscriber ratio: 1% or higher
- First 24-hour performance: 15% above channel baseline
